Small Business Invoice Checklist

A small business invoice should look professional and include enough information for the client to approve payment without asking extra questions.

Checklist

Include your logo, business name, client name, invoice number, invoice date, due date, item list, tax, discount, shipping or other fees, total, amount paid and balance due.

Payment details

Add payment methods such as bank transfer, card, PayPal, Wise or Stripe. For international clients, choose the correct currency symbol and confirm payment fees when needed.

Record keeping

Save the PDF invoice and keep a copy of the final amount in your bookkeeping records. Consistent invoice numbers make tracking easier.
