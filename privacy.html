Privacy Policy

InvoiceGen Pro is a browser-based free invoice generator. The invoice details you type into the form are used to update the preview on your device and create a printable or downloadable invoice.

Information you enter

Business names, client names, addresses, item descriptions, prices, notes and uploaded logos are processed in your browser for the invoice preview. We do not require account registration to use the tool.

Logo uploads

Uploaded logo files are used only to display the logo in the invoice preview. Avoid uploading sensitive images or documents that are not needed for your invoice.

Third-party libraries

The site may load trusted CDN libraries for PDF generation and image rendering. These services can receive technical browser data such as IP address, user agent and request time according to their own policies.

Analytics and advertising

If analytics or advertising is added later, it may use cookies or similar technologies. This policy will be updated when those services are enabled.
