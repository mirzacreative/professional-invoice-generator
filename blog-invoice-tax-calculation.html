How Invoice Tax Calculation Works

Most invoices start with a subtotal. The subtotal is calculated by multiplying quantity and rate for each line item, then adding all line totals together.

Tax and discounts

Tax is usually applied as a percentage of the subtotal. Discounts may also be entered as a percentage, reducing the total before the final balance is shown. Always check local tax rules before sending an invoice.

Advance payments

If the client already paid a deposit, show it as advance paid. This makes the balance due clear and helps both sides keep better records.

Example

If the subtotal is 100, tax is 10 percent and discount is 5 percent, the invoice shows each adjustment separately so the client can understand the final total.
